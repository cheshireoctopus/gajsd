Feedr Lab: Getting Started

• Each feed article should be rendered as such:


  <article class="article">
    <section class="featuredImage">
      <img src="{{image}}" aslt="" />
    </section>
    <section class="articleContent">
        <a href="{{link}}" target="_blank"><h3>{{title}}</h3></a>
        <h6>{{type}}</h6>
    </section>
    <section class="published">
      {{date}}
    </section>
    <div class="clearfix"></div>
  </article>

• Some articles may not have image/category
